### 7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

We recognize and respect your rights regarding your personal information. Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

**Right to Access (Article 15 GDPR):**
* Request access to the personal information we hold about you
* Obtain information about how we process it

**Right to Rectification (Article 16 GDPR):**
* Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information

**Right to Erasure (Article 17 GDPR):**
* Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
* Applies when data is no longer necessary or you withdraw consent

**Right to Restriction (Article 18 GDPR):**
* Request that we limit processing of your personal information

**Right to Data Portability (Article 20 GDPR):**
* Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
* Transmit your data to another service provider

**Right to Object (Article 21 GDPR):**
* Object to processing of your personal information, including for direct marketing

**Right to Withdraw Consent (Article 7(3) GDPR):**
* Revoke consent for data processing at any time
* Does not affect lawfulness of processing before withdrawal

**Right to Lodge a Complaint (Article 77 GDPR):**
* File a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe our processing violates applicable laws

**Right to Non-Discrimination:**
* Protected from discrimination for exercising your rights

**Right to Correct:**
* Request correction of inaccurate information we have about you

**Right to Limit:**
* Request we only use sensitive personal information for limited purposes

**Right to Opt-Out:**
* Request that we stop processing your personal information

**Exercising Your Rights:**
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:
* Email: [legal@starlit.you](mailto:legal@starlit.you)
* Phone: +1-US 3612462043

We will respond to your request within **30 days** in accordance with applicable data protection laws. We may need to verify your identity to ensure the security of your personal information.

**Appeals:**

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:
